Model Overview

Full push, fuel-saving stints, tyre-change penalties and chaos factor are combined into a simple but powerful prediction engine. The GA solver evaluates strategies across stop counts to find the best total laps in the race window.

Plan A is the full-push benchmark. Plan B is the best GA result across stop counts. Plan C is a constrained GA with one fewer stop than Plan A.
